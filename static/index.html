Why Buy a Japanese Import?

If you are looking for a reliable, spacious family vehicle or a unique car with unmatched specifications, Japanese imports offer unparalleled value.

Exceptional Condition

Because roads in Japan are largely salt-free in winter and vehicles face strict, mandatory domestic testing, these cars arrive in the UK in extraordinary rust-free condition.

Remarkably Low Mileage

Japanese imports consistently arrive with significantly fewer miles on the clock than equivalent UK-market vehicles, meaning a fresher engine and longer lifespan.

High Specification

Packed with premium features, advanced technology, luxury touches, and comfortable interiors you simply won't find in local market equivalents.
